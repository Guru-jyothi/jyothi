About Us

Jyothi Vidya Sangha (R), Peraje

Founded in 1973 and managed by Jyothi Vidya Sangha (R), Peraje, Jyothi High School has been providing quality education to over 5,000 children from rural Kodagu and Dakshina Kannada districts for 45 years. Located in Peraje village, Kodagu District, near the Western Ghats, the school is situated on a 2.75-acre campus. It currently serves more than 100 students in grades 8 to 10, supported by a dedicated team consisting of 1 headmaster, 6 teaching staff, and 2 non-teaching staff.

However, over the years, the school has faced challenges such as inadequate infrastructure, lack of transportation, and limited modern educational facilities, which have led to a decline in student admissions. In response, Pranav Foundation has signed an MoU with Jyothi Vidya Sangha to revitalize the school’s infrastructure and integrate advanced educational methods. This collaboration aims to better serve underprivileged communities and align with Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education).

Pranav Foundation

Founded on April 27th, 2021, right before the second COVID-19 lockdown, Pranav Foundation swiftly addressed the pressing needs of the community across health, education, environment, and cultural sectors.

In education, we’ve transformed 46 government school classrooms into digital learning hubs, reaching over 25,000 students. We’ve also set up a computer lab at Govt High School, benefiting over 200 students annually. Pranav Foundation supports underprivileged children in government schools by providing essential educational supplies. These include notebooks, writing materials, exam pads, and reusable sustainable pads for girls. To date, we’ve distributed over 5,000 notebooks, 1,000 exam pads, 5,000 stationery items, and 1,000 reusable pads to government schools in both Bengaluru Rural & Urban areas, as well as the Kodagu district.

Key Benefits

The goal of Jyothi Vidya Sangha is to provide comprehensive education integrated with technology to equip children to face the challenges of the modern world. Along with lessons and play, the school offers innovative teaching methods and technological facilities.

Modern Laboratory

To nurture critical thinking among children, the teaching of science is essential. The science laboratory stimulates curiosity and interest in children, encouraging them to pursue scientific education.

Digital Education

Mastering computers and modern technology is vital in today's world. Children are taught essential tech skills that build a strong foundation for their future careers, helping them thrive in a digital era.

Encouragement for Sports

Sports are as important as academics, aiding mental agility and physical growth. The school encourages active participation in sports and supports children in becoming skilled athletes.

English-Speaking Classes

Speaking English is tough for many children, and for some, it feels impossible. Special classes help them learn to speak fluently and confidently, overcoming fear and hesitation.

Free Learning Materials

Children receive all essential learning materials, such as pencils, pens, erasers, compass boxes, and notebooks, free of cost, ensuring they never need to worry about acquiring supplies.

Free Uniforms

All children attending the school are considered equal. To ensure uniformity, neat and clean uniforms are provided, which help boost children's confidence and foster a sense of equality.

Educational Trips

Education goes beyond four walls; for children, the world is a classroom. To enrich their learning, two free educational trips are arranged yearly to explore historical and archaeological sites.

Purified Drinking Water

The school prioritizes the health and well-being of children by offering clean, safe drinking water, ensuring they stay hydrated and healthy while focusing on their education and overall growth.
